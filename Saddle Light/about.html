BRIEF HISTORY

Saddle Light Foundation is a one man Non-governmental organization that was founded in April, 2022 by Mr. Adeyemi Salau solely for the impacting of technological knowledge to various individuals as low as 8 years old.

In this 21st century, technology is advancing drastically, hence to be a part of over 1000+ organizations that desire to teach or pass knowledge to individuals who are interested in the technological aspect in this world, hence making our world a technology – advanced world in various aspects.

At Saddle Light we dwell solely on software development, website development, mobile app development, UI/UX design, data analytics.

We aspire to see a generation that is technology – driven especially the young individuals.

MISSION

To bring Saddle Light Foundation into a recognised institution that instructs or impats technological ideas into individuals.

VISION

To become a global technological institution impacting knowledge to all kinds of generations to become versatile in the technological world.
